Features

Everything your resume
needs to get noticed.

Resync doesn't just add keywords — it understands your experience and places them where they land hardest.

01
Keyword Extraction & Scoring
Resync scans job descriptions and extracts every critical keyword — required, preferred, and contextual — ranked by importance to the role.
02
AI Bullet Rewriting
See your original bullet and an AI-rewritten version side by side. Accept, edit, or reject each change with a single click — you're always in control.
03
Contextual Match Detection
Not all keywords are a direct match. Resync identifies when your implied experience covers a keyword and asks one smart clarifying question before proceeding.
04
ATS Score Optimization
Applicant Tracking Systems filter out 75% of resumes before a human ever sees them. Resync ensures your document passes every ATS scan for the job you're targeting.
05
Cover Letter Generator
Once your resume is tailored, generate a matching cover letter in seconds — personalized to the job, reflecting your newly synced experience.
06
Live Progress Dashboard
Track exactly how many keywords are integrated, pending, matched, or not applicable — a real-time readout of your resume's strength against any job posting.
